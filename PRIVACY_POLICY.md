## 4. Data Storage & Synchronization

### 4.1 Cloud Storage
Your personal information and content are stored on **Firebase Firestore**, Google's cloud database service. This ensures:
- Automatic backups
- Data synchronization across devices
- Real-time access to your tasks and notes

### 4.2 Data Retention
- **Active Account:** Data is retained for as long as your account is active
- **Deleted Account:** Your data will be deleted within 30 days of account deletion
- **Backup Copies:** Backup copies may persist in Firebase recovery systems for up to 90 days

### 4.3 Offline Access
The App may store cached copies of your data on your device for offline access. User can clear app data to remove cached information at any time.
